WHO WE ARE —

Morwenna are shipowners and operators working worldwide with specialist knowledge in the Caspian providing towing and pusher tugs, barges, anchor handlers, AHTS, ice breakers and accommodation vessels. We have over 20 years of experience in arranging towage of all types including heavy lift, ro-ro and outsize cargo shipments and offer unrivaled experience in handling our clients’ projects through the Russian Inland Waterways System.
